THIS PAGE IS ABOUT MY CAT MINMIN

About Minmin

We adopted her 2 years ago when she was only 6 weeks old. Her mom gave birth to her along with 4 of her siblings. We had been wanting a cat for a while and when I saw someone posted that they are giving kittens away our desired came to fruition. Some of my family members didn't want her at first, but they started liking and petting her after we got her.

She is a black and white cat; we wish we knew her breed. What makes her physically different than other cats is her symmetric bow on the nose. She is small but don't underestimate her power. She can be feisty if you try to bother her or make her do something she doesn't feel like doing. She scratches us sometimes, but we love her.

Below is a picture of hers. Enjoy seeing a beautiful face.

Features and Characteristics

  • Symmetric bow on the nose
  • Small but strong
  • Likes going outside. Runs back in if there's any loud sound
    • Especially, she loves going on the roof

Things she likes:

  1. Treat! Treat! Treat!
  2. Drinking someone else's water whether it be our water glass or my cousin's cat's water bowl
  3. Sleeping on my favorite blanket. She wouldn't sleep in her bed without the blanket on top

Now let's talk about things she hates:

  1. People bothering her when she is chilling
  2. When she is outside and we want her to be in
  3. Strangers: that basically excludes everyone except me, my younger siblings, and my mom
